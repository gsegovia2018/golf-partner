## New Playwright Findings

1. P1 - Floating tab bar occludes content. On Play desktop, the All caught up title/body sit behind the floating tab bar. On History desktop and mobile, game cards are visibly covered by the floating tab bar. This is a real viewport issue, not just a source-level concern. Fix with bottom padding/inset equal to tab height plus safe-area spacing on every tab scroll container, and verify empty states place final copy above the bar.

2. P1 - Inactive tabs remain in the accessibility snapshot. After navigating from Play to History to Feed, Playwright's accessibility snapshot still contained Home, History, and Feed trees together. Opening the Home menu also left the background route content exposed under the dialog in the snapshot. Fix by ensuring inactive screens and modal backdrops are hidden from accessibility on web/native: accessibilityElementsHidden, importantForAccessibility="no-hide-descendants", inert/aria-hidden equivalents where applicable.

3. P2 - Home action-card contrast is weaker than expected. Rendered contrast checks found muted text at roughly 3.16-3.50:1 and black-on-green active surfaces around 3.03:1 in the Home view. This confirms the token-level contrast concern from the source pass. Use white or a much lighter foreground on green surfaces and raise muted copy contrast.

4. P2 - My Stats horizontal tabs are scrollable but partially hidden on mobile. This appears intentional, but the last tab is clipped with no strong affordance that more tabs are available. Consider a subtle fade/edge affordance or reduce tab count in the first row.

## Revised Priority Order

1. Fix bottom inset/padding for tab content so the floating nav never covers cards or empty-state copy.
2. Hide inactive routes and modal backgrounds from the accessibility tree.
3. Repair contrast tokens and active green foreground colors.
4. Keep My Stats report-card-first; apply the earlier Stats simplification to deeper analytics views, not necessarily this default screen.
5. Continue with scorecard distillation from the source review; Playwright did not reach a live scorecard in this pass.
