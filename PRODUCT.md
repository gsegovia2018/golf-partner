# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Golf Partner is for a small group of friends who play weekend golf games and multi-round tournaments together. They use the app before a round to set up teams and courses, during play to score quickly, and afterwards to see what friends played, who scored well, and what photos or memories were added.

## Product Purpose

The product keeps casual golf scoring organized without making the group feel like they are operating tournament software. Success means players can start a game, record Stableford scores, follow friend activity, revisit finished rounds, and share memories with low friction on web and Android.

## Brand Personality

Casual clubhouse: social, familiar, lightly competitive. The interface should feel like checking in on friends after a round, with scorekeeping available when needed rather than dominating every surface.

## Anti-references

Avoid enterprise sports admin dashboards, sterile spreadsheet-like leaderboards, generic social feeds that hide the golf context, and decorative golf nostalgia that slows down scoring workflows.

## Design Principles

- Lead with people and recency before raw numbers.
- Keep scoring details close, but do not make every screen feel like a scorecard.
- Preserve friendly rivalry through ranks, reactions, comments, and small callouts.
- Respect mobile use at the course: compact controls, clear tap targets, and readable summaries outdoors.
- Use familiar product patterns so the app feels reliable when people are mid-round.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Do not rely on color alone for score quality, rankings, selected filters, or error states. Motion should be brief and state-driven, with reduced-motion behavior where animation is introduced.
